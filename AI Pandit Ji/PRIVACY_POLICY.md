# AI Pandit Ji Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 18, 2026_

AI Pandit Ji provides kundli matching using birth details, uploaded kundli files, OCR processing, and generated match reports.

## Data You Enter

The app may process:

- names entered for matching
- date of birth
- time of birth
- birth place
- uploaded kundli images or PDFs
- manually entered kundli values such as rashi, nakshatra, lagna, and notes

## How Data Is Used

This data is used only to:

- calculate kundli match results
- read kundli images or PDFs through OCR
- generate on-screen results
- generate downloadable match report cards

## External Services

The app may contact external services for:

- OCR processing through OCR.space
- place lookup and timezone resolution through Open-Meteo and OpenStreetMap Nominatim

These services may receive the data needed to complete those features.

## Local Storage

The app may store the latest form values locally on the device or browser to help users continue where they left off.

## Data Sharing

AI Pandit Ji does not intentionally sell user data.

## Data Retention

Generated values and temporary form data may remain on the local device until the user clears app or browser storage.

## Children

This app is not designed specifically for children.
